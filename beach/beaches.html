Tepanee Beach

Tepanee is located on a small hill that rises from the south part of Logon Beach and descends on our small private beach facing southwest, an area isolated from the rest of the island from which you can admire breathtaking sunsets and where we have arranged umbrellas, sunbeds and Chiringuito restaurant bar. In addition, in the hill adjacent to the reception is located our panoramic Amihan restaurant.

Shangri-La Mactan

Shangri-La's Mactan Resort and Spa, Cebu is a luxurious resort located in a picturesque tropical setting in the Philippines. The resort offers a wide range of dining, entertainment, sports, and recreational activities. The resort is situated on 13 hectares of lush greenery with landscaped gardens and a pristine white-sand beach, it is also a modern-day paradise cherished by both domestic and international travelers.

Dusit Thani

Dusit Thani Mactan Cebu is a vibrant and inviting retreat. Blending Thai and Filipino warmth and hospitality, guests will enjoy thoughtful service, innovative cuisine, and stunning views. Refreshing tradewinds waft throughout the resort, from the open-air lobby and lounge, to stylish dining venues offering indoor and al fresco seating, all overlooking our signature pools and the blue waters of the Magellan Bay. It is a place to visit for such view.
